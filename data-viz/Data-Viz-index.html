By Philip Van Slooten

ACFT Fails by Gender: 2019

The Army is moving forward with implementing a new "gender neutral" combat fitness test despite initial reports of a high failure rate by female troops.

Military.com reported in October the female failure rate was as high as 84 percent according to "leaked slides" revealing the results of "11 batallions, which included 2,849 men and 357 women."



Note: 1 - Deadlift fails; 2 - Standing Power Throw fails; 3 - Hand Release Push Up Fails; 4 - Sprint, Drag, Carry fails; 5 - Leg Tuck fails and 6 - 2 Mile Run fails. Source: Military.com and armytimes.com
